  1. Ensure your device is rooted with Magisk installed.
  2. Download the ReVanced YouTube Music Magisk zip file.
  3. Open Magisk Manager on your device.
  4. Tap on the 'Modules' section.

  6. Navigate to and select the downloaded zip file.
  7. Wait for the installation to complete.
