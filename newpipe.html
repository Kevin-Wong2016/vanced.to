NewPipe Features

  • Lightweight: NewPipe is a smaller app compared to the official YouTube app, consuming fewer resources on your device.
  • Offline Usage: NewPipe allows you to download videos and audio directly to your device for watching or listening without an internet connection.
  • Extended Privacy: Unlike the official YouTube app, NewPipe prioritizes user privacy. It doesn't use Google libraries or require a Google account to function, making it suitable for users concerned about data collection.
  • Background Player: You can listen to audio from videos in the background while using other apps on your device.
  • Popup Player: NewPipe offers a popup window to play videos in a smaller resizable window, allowing you to multitask more easily.
  • Subscriptions: Similar to YouTube, NewPipe lets you subscribe to your favorite channels to stay updated on their latest content.
  • Bookmarks: NewPipe allows you to bookmark videos for easy access later, similar to how you might bookmark websites in a web browser.
  • History: NewPipe keeps track of your watch history, making it easier to revisit videos you've watched previously.
