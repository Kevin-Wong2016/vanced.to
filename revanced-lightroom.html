What is ReVanced Lightroom

ReVanced Lightroom is a modded version of the popular Adobe Lightroom app, designed to provide users with access to all premium features without cost. This modified app removes ads, unlocks advanced editing tools, and provides unlimited cloud storage. Users can enjoy professional-grade photo editing capabilities, including RAW editing, HDR merging, lens correction, color grading, and batch editing.

ReVanced Lightroom aims to make high-quality photo editing accessible to all users, offering the full range of Lightroom's powerful features in a free package.
