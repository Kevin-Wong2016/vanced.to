ReVanced YouTube Extended - RVX - Android 6 & Android 7

ReVanced Youtube Extended (RVX) is a modified version of the official YouTube app that adds a number of features and customization options. It is an open-source project that is developed by a community of volunteers. This patch is for Android 6 & Android 7

ReVanced YouTube Extended Features

  • Support for Android 6 and Android 7: Compatible with Android 6.0 Marshmallow to 7.1.2 Nougat.
  • ReVanced Extended fork for YouTube 17.34.36: An extended fork version specifically designed for YouTube 17.34.36.
  • Improved Interface: Enhanced user interface features such as change seekbar color in dark mode, addition of more video speed options, ability to set default video quality and speed settings.
  • Disable Features: Disable haptic feedback when swiping, disable landscape mode when entering fullscreen, disable the "Update your app" screen, disable notification when you launch background play for the first time.
  • Playback Controls: Enables minimized and background playback, enables the original quality flyout menu, enables tap-to-seek on the seekbar, enables the tablet mini player layout.
  • Additional Options: Adds options to hide various components such as action buttons under a video, comment component under a video, player settings flyout panel, tooltip box, video description and comments panel in fullscreen view, live chat button in the video player, captions button in the video player, next prev button in the player controller, creator's watermarks on videos, autoplay button in the video player, cast button in the video player, email address in the account switcher
  • Ad Removal: Removes ads in the video player for a seamless streaming experience.
  • Advanced Features: Allows YouTube ReVanced to run without root and under a different package name with Vanced MicroG, adds missing translation files from YouTube, adds volume and brightness swipe controls.
  • Integration with External Services: Integrates SponsorBlock which allows skipping video segments such as sponsored content.
  • Custom themes: Offers a variety of themes with custom application, including a default amoled theme.
  • Crowdin Translations: Multilingual support through crowd-sourced translations on Crowdin.
